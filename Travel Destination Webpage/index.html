Jaipur is the finest jewelry ever created on earth, where king was the jeweler and bricks his gems

Location : Jaipur is situated in the east-central part of Rajasthan, roughly equidistant from Alwar and Ajmer. It lies at 26° 55' north latitude and 75° 49' east longitude.

Culture : Jaipur is renowned for its colorful and vibrant culture, attracting tourists from around the world.
It is known as the "Pink City" due to the distinctive pink color of its historic buildings, particularly in the old city.

Why it's interesting : Exceptional Architecture: Jaipur boasts an extraordinary blend of Rajput and Mughal architecture, evident in its numerous palaces and forts.

Famous Places

  • Amer Fort. A Majestic Hilltop Fort.
  • Hawa Mahal. A Five-Storeyed Historic Landmark.
  • Nahargarh Fort. The majestic abode of tigers!
  • City Palace. The Residence of the Royal family.
  • Birla Mandir. Discover Peace at Birla Mandir.
